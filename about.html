Who are we?

Snap, List, Sell.
Omniscience is a online marketplace that makes selling your old books as simple as taking a photo. We are on a mission to make online buying and selling accessible to all while creating a safe, friendly and helpful community of happy users!

We have a vision to change the world with knowledge, with our tag-line the
"The compass of the knowledge seekers"
we want people to reuse such vessels of knowledge to better our society, and let it be a beacon of guiding light for the knowledge seekers

Omniscience is a non-profit platform, therefore rest assured for there will be no commision charges or whatsoever, we simply want our vistors to just sit back and enjoy the experience of shopping and selling with us.
